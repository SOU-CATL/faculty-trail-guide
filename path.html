Pick Your Path

Two ways to make the hike — choose the one that fits your summer.

Two Ways to Trek

🏕️
Community Path

Trail Crew

You like the social aspect. We meet at "Base Camp" (Zoom or on campus) on a schedule. We move through the modules together, clear the path together, and share what we're learning about our own courses.

Good for you if:

  • You want accountability and enjoy talking shop with colleagues
  • You want real-time help when you get stuck
  • You learn better in community
See Base Camp Dates →
🎒
Independent Path

Solo Section-Hiker

You've got the Field Guide in your pack. You dip in when you have a free hour, tackle one Trail Improvement at a time, and hike at your own pace. No required meetings — just progress.

Good for you if:

  • Your summer schedule is unpredictable
  • You prefer to work independently
  • You're already comfortable with some of the tools
