MODULE 2

Clearing the Overgrowth

Brickfield Accessibility Toolkit

The Trail Metaphor

Your Trail Maintenance Crew

Every course has fallen logs — PDFs that screen readers can't parse, Word docs that look fine on your screen but are completely inaccessible to a student with a visual processing difference. Brickfield is your trail maintenance crew. It shows you what's broken. Then it helps you fix it.

The UDL Connection

Accessible Materials Benefit Everyone

Accessible course materials benefit all learners, but they are essential for:

  • Students with dyslexia who prefer or need audio versions of text
  • Students with visual processing differences or low vision
  • Screen reader users
  • Students with executive function challenges who need cleaner, more structured content

Fixing a broken PDF is not just a technical task. It is the difference between a student who can access your course and one who cannot.

Your Trail Activity

Three Steps to a Clearer Path

1
Run the Brickfield accessibility audit on one of your Moodle courses.
2
Fix the three highest-priority errors. The toolkit tells you exactly what they are and how to fix each one.
3
Convert at least one inaccessible document (PDF or Word) into an accessible format using the document converter.
